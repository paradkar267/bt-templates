Understanding Skin Profiles

T-Zone Shine & Breakouts

Oily & Acne-Prone

Characterized by excess sebum, enlarged pores, and frequent blemishes. Requires balancing and non-comedogenic hydration.

Sun Spots & Uneven Tone

Hyperpigmentation

Dark patches caused by sun exposure or post-inflammatory marks. Needs brightening agents like Vitamin C and diligent SPF.

Fine Lines & Loss of Elasticity

Mature & Dry

Lacks moisture and natural oils, showing fine lines. Thrives on rich ceramides, peptides, and deep moisturization.

Redness & Reactivity

Sensitive & Reactive

Easily irritated with visible redness or compromised barrier. Benefits from soothing ingredients like Centella and Aloe.
