FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common urgent care questions.

What should I do in a medication emergency?

Call Poison Control at 1-800-222-1222 immediately. If someone is unconscious or not breathing, call 911 first. Our 24/7 pharmacist line can also provide immediate guidance on accidental overdose, drug interactions, and adverse reactions.

Can I get emergency prescription refills after hours?

Yes! Our Express Rx Refill system operates 24/7. For controlled substances, a pharmacist will verify your identity and prescription history. Most emergency refills are ready within 30 minutes at our 24-hour locations.

Do you accept walk-in patients for vaccines?

Absolutely. We offer walk-in vaccinations for flu, COVID-19, shingles, pneumonia, and travel immunizations at all locations during operating hours. No appointment necessary — just bring your insurance card and ID.

How does the insurance pricing tool work?

Enter your medication name and insurance plan, and we'll instantly show your copay, out-of-pocket cost, and cheaper generic alternatives. We partner with GoodRx and all major insurers to find you the lowest price available.

Is the Live Pharmacist Chat free?

Yes, our Live Pharmacist Chat is completely free for all users. You can ask about medication interactions, dosage questions, side effects, and general health advice. For detailed consultations, we recommend booking a paid appointment.
